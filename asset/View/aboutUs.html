The Pirates Hotel with world-class facilities and services is an ideal choice for guests on business trips and vacations when visiting the most vibrant and bustling city in Vietnam.

Currency

You can exchange cash and checks at banks or money changers. Equatorial Hotel TP. Ho Chi Minh City, major hotels and shopping centers accept Master Card and Visa cards. Please check with staff before making a purchase as some smaller establishments may not accept credit cards. The US Dollar is the most preferred currency, so you should bring US Dollars. The currencies of England, Australia, Japan, France, Germany, Belgium, Netherlands, Singapore and Thailand can be exchanged in major cities (such as Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi, Danang, Hai Phong).

Visa

All international tourists visiting Vietnam need a visa. Entry visas can be obtained at Vietnam embassies, consulates or travel agents. The visa process takes about five working days, once a visa has been granted, you must enter the country within 90 days. Tourist visas can be entered once or multiple times and business visas can be obtained through the sponsoring agency of Vietnam, valid for a maximum of six months and allow multiple entry. If the visa is a separate one, copy and separate it from your passport.

Skin

You can wear casual, comfortable and polite clothes. Wearing shoulders and feet should not be worn when visiting temples, pagodas or churches.

Health care

Medical services in the city. Ho Chi Minh City is relatively good, but out of the big cities, medical services may not be guaranteed. Consult your doctor about health requirements before the trip.

Water

Apart from boiled water, tap water is the most consumed. Bottled water and mineral water are also popular.

Tips

Tips are not required but are very much appreciated. Some restaurants charge an additional 10% -15% on service charges and Value Added Tax. A tip of about 0.5 - 1 USD is considered suitable for room service staff or luggage staff.

Public transport

Metered taxis are very popular and affordable, especially in the city. Ho Chi Minh City, where there are many taxi companies to choose from. You can also choose to rent a car with driver service in major cities for $ 25 to $ 100 a day.

Mobile phone

GSM network is currently being used in Vietnam.

Airport

International airports in Vietnam include: Tan Son Nhat Airport (SGN) in Ho Chi Minh City. Ho Chi Minh City, Da Nang International Airport (DAD) in Ho Chi Minh City Danang and Noi Bai International Airport (HAN) in Hanoi

Domestic airport

Buon Ma Thuot Airport (BMV) and Cam Ranh Airport (VVCR)

Airport tax

International departure tax is USD 14 and domestic departure is VND 15,000 to VND 20,000 (approximately USD 1).
